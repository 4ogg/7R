About OpenFF7R

Making Final Fantasy VII Remake modding accessible to everyone through community-driven tools and resources.

OpenFF7R is a dedicated community focused on making modding for Final Fantasy VII Remake accessible to everyone. Whether you're an experienced modder or just getting started, our tools and community are here to help you create and enjoy amazing mods for the FF7 Remake series on PC.

Our mission is to:

  • Break down barriers to modding through user-friendly tools and comprehensive documentation
  • Foster creativity in the FF7R community by providing powerful and flexible modding capabilities
  • Develop and maintain open-source modding tools that evolve with the community's needs
  • Create comprehensive documentation for modders of all skill levels
  • Provide support for modders through our active Discord community

Join us in expanding the possibilities of FF7 Remake on PC and become part of our growing community of passionate modders!

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FF7R modding

Do I need to own the game to use these mods?

Yes, you need to own a legitimate copy of Final Fantasy VII Remake on PC to use our mods. Our community does not support or encourage piracy.

I'm new to modding. Is this community beginner-friendly?

Absolutely! Our community is specifically focused on making modding accessible to everyone, regardless of experience level. We have dedicated channels for beginners and plenty of members willing to help you get started.

Will mods affect my game's performance?

It depends on the type of mod. Some mods might improve performance, while others, especially high-resolution textures or complex gameplay changes, might impact performance. Each mod in our community typically includes information about potential performance impacts.

Can I use mods with the Epic Games Store version?

Yes, our tools and mods work with both the Steam and Epic Games Store versions of Final Fantasy VII Remake. Installation methods may vary slightly between platforms, but we provide guides for both.

How do I contribute to the community?

There are many ways to contribute! You can create mods, help answer questions from other members, contribute to documentation, or even help test new mods and tools. Join our Discord and let us know your interests and skills!
